Interesting Facts from 2024

• In 2024, seven different drivers won at least one Grand Prix, and it was the first time since 2012 that so many winners appeared.
• Both Lando Norris and Oscar Piastri scored their first-ever F1 wins during the season.
• Lewis Hamilton ended a long win drought by winning at Silverstone (his 104th career win).
• 2024 was the first season in Formula 1 history in which four different constructors each recorded a 1–2 finish in a Grand Prix (Red Bull, Ferrari, McLaren, Mercedes).
• Midseason, Verstappen tied Ayrton Senna's all-time record of eight consecutive pole positions.
